About


I am currently a visiting scholar in the IFP (Image Formation and Processing) group at Beckman Institute, University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), advised by Prof. Thomas S. Huang. I am also a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Qingxiong Yang and Prof. Rynson W. H. Lau. My research interests include computer vision, computer graphics, and machine learning. I am particularly interested in 3D vision and low-level vision. I also work on learning better representations by exploring auxiliary from different modalities.
